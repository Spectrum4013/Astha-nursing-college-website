About Us

Educating nurses for the hospitals that need them most.

Aastha School of Nursing was established in 2011 as a General Nursing & Midwifery (GNM) school serving the Dharashiv (Osmanabad) district. We exist for a simple reason: this region needs more trained nurses, and the students here deserve a college that doesn't price them out.

We're affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board of Nursing & Paramedical Education (MSBNPE), run by Shri Sharada Shikshan Sanstha, with an approved annual intake of 20 seats for the GNM programme.

Courses & Services

The GNM Diploma — 3 years, subject by subject.

General Nursing & Midwifery (GNM) is a 3-year diploma course. Here's exactly what's covered, year by year.

01

First Year

  • Bio-Sciences
  • Behavioural Sciences
  • Nursing Foundation
  • Community Health Nursing – I
02

Second Year

  • Medical-Surgical Nursing – I
  • Medical-Surgical Nursing – II
  • Mental Health Nursing
  • Paediatric Nursing
03

Third Year

  • Nursing Management
  • Midwifery & Gynaecological Nursing
  • Community Health Nursing – II
  • Nursing Education & Intro to Research
  • Professional Trends
